The Process

How It Works

Seven steps. One real experience.

Apply

Fill out the application with your background, domain preference, and availability. No entrance test. No prior experience required.

01

Telephonic Interview

Shortlisted applicants get a 10–15 minute call. We want to know if you're serious, available, and ready to work in a team.

02

Get Placed in a Team

Every domain runs in small teams of 4–6 with a Team Lead and Co-Team Lead. Everyone owns a specific module.

03

Weekly Team Sync

Progress updates, blockers, task assignments. Outside of sync — everything is async. Just weekly accountability.

04

Own Your Module

Push to GitHub. Communicate on Slack. Your mentor is available async. Your team depends on your output being ready.

05

Ship the Capstone

Your team presents the completed project. Your mentor reviews individual contribution and signs off.

06

Walk Away With More

A real project in your portfolio, a team you built with, and a verifiable credential any employer can authenticate.
